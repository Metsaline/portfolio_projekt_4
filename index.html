Introduction

Sydney Jay Mead was an American industrial designer and neo-futurist concept artist, widely known for his designs for science-fiction films such as Blade Runner, Aliens and Tron. Mead has been described as "the artist who illustrates the future" and "one of the most influential concept artists and industrial designers of our time. Born in July 18, 1933 Saint Paul, Minnesota, U.S. and Died December 30, 2019 (age 86) Pasadena, California, U.S.

ADG William Cameron Menzies Award (2020) - The award recognizes his innovative neo-futuristic concept artwork on numerous legendary movies.
Spectrum Award for Grand Master (2007) - Got for working as an artist for more than 20 years and have influenced other artists.
London Critics Circle Film Award for Special Achievement (1983) - Best Special Effects
